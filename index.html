Expertise Summary

Strategic Data Analysis & Insight Generation (SQL, Power BI, Excel)

I apply advanced analytical techniques using SQL, Power BI, and Excel to uncover trends, identify performance gaps, and support data-driven decision-making. With SQL, I extract and manipulate large datasets efficiently; Excel enables exploratory analysis and modeling, while Power BI translates raw data into strategic, actionable insights through intuitive dashboards.

Data Transformation & Visual Storytelling (Power Query, Power BI, Excel)

I use Power Query, Power BI, and Excel, I transform messy, unstructured data into clean, analysis-ready formats. I automate cleansing and reshaping tasks with Power Query, then integrate visuals in Power BI and Excel to create compelling narratives that make data easy to interpret and act on. The result is not just visual appeal but clarity that drives business impact.

Process Automation & Operational Efficiency (Power Automate, Power BI, Excel)

I design and deploy automated workflows with Power Automate that eliminate repetitive manual tasks and streamline reporting cycles. These workflows connect tools like Power BI and Excel, ensuring real-time data updates, automated alerts, and seamless integration across departments. This automation reduces human error and accelerates insight delivery.

Data Communication & Stakeholder Engagement (Power BI, Excel, SQL)

I bridge the gap between data and decision-makers by delivering insights in a clear, concise, and tailored format. Through interactive Power BI dashboards, Excel reports, and thoughtful SQL-driven summaries, I ensure stakeholders at all levels can engage with the data, ask the right questions, and take confident, informed actions.
